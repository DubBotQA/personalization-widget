Reading Guide Test Section

This section contains multiple lines of text to test the Reading Guide feature. Enable the Reading Guide from the personalization widget and move your mouse over this text.

The Reading Guide creates a horizontal strip that follows your cursor, highlighting the current line while dimming the rest of the content. This can be particularly helpful for users with reading difficulties or visual tracking issues.

Try moving your mouse up and down through these paragraphs with the Reading Guide enabled. You should see a clear horizontal band that moves with your cursor, making it easier to focus on one line at a time.

The Reading Guide can be toggled on and off easily using the switch in the personalization widget, and your preference will be saved for future visits to the page.
